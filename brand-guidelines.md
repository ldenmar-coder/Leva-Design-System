# Leva Brand Guidelines

The single source of truth for Leva Sleep brand standards in email and landing page work. Every campaign, flow, and LP build should conform to this document.

---

## Colors

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Deep Purple | `#2D1B4E` | Primary brand color. Backgrounds, headlines, primary buttons. |
| Gold | `#E8B547` | Accent. CTAs, highlights, dividers, key numbers. Use sparingly for emphasis. |
| Cream | `#F5F1E8` | Light background, section backgrounds, body areas. |
| Lavender | `#F4F1F8` | Soft secondary background, cards, subtle blocks. |

Keep contrast accessible: gold on deep purple is the signature pairing; avoid gold text on cream (low contrast).

---

## Typography

- **Campaign emails:** Helvetica Neue
- **Demo Days emails:** Nunito

Use the family consistently within a single email. Do not mix the two systems in one build.

---

## Voice and tone

Leva is calm, premium, and reassuring — the opposite of hype. We sell better sleep and a better life, not a product spec sheet.

**Messaging framework (Rik's): Attract / Engage / Connect**
- **Attract** — lead with the outcome or the felt problem, not the product.
- **Engage** — build the case with proof and mechanism.
- **Connect** — tie it back to the guest's life and invite the next step.

---

## Copy conventions

These are hard rules. Claude Design and any AI-assisted draft must follow them.

- **No em dashes.** Use a period, comma, or restructure the sentence.
- **No exclamation marks.** Confidence comes from calm, not punctuation.
- **"Guests," not "customers."** Always.
- **Sign-off:** `Elevate yourself.`
- **Guarantee naming:** always written as the **60 Night Sleep-On-It Guarantee**.

---

## Pricing presentation

- **Lead with monthly pricing, never the total.** Format: `from $X/mo, $0 down`.
- Never bake sale prices, discount percentages, or promo codes into static images (they go stale and can't be corrected after send).
- Never use a stale or wrong discount code. Confirm the active code before every send.

---

## Product positioning

**The product is not the hero.** Lead with the guest's outcome — better sleep, less snoring, relief from pain, more connection with a partner. The bed is the mechanism that delivers it, introduced after the outcome is established.

---

## Approval gates

- **Comparative claims** that name competitors (Tempur-Pedic, Stearns & Foster, Kingsdown) require **Matt's approval before running.**
- Only use claims from the Approved Claims library. Do not invent statistics or proof points.
- Source all product URLs and image URLs from the live store. Do not fabricate URLs.

---

## Technical / asset notes

- **Shopify CDN base:** `https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0256/8270/6495/`
- All image URLs should resolve from the live store CDN. The Asset Master is the lookup for approved image URLs.
- Avoid base64-embedded images (they bloat file size and hurt deliverability).
