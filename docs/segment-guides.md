# Leva Sleep Segment Guides

Core problem, messaging pillars, strong angles, what to avoid, and best product fits for each audience segment. Pair with the Subject Line Library and Product Positioning Guide when building.

---

## Snoring

**Core problem:** Guests are losing sleep because snoring disrupts one or both partners.

**Messaging pillars:** Better airflow · Upper-body elevation · Independent adjustability · Better sleep for both partners · Less disruption throughout the night

**Strong angles:**
- Better airflow. Better sleep.
- Stop waking each other up.
- Sleep together. Comfortably.
- The angle matters.

**Avoid:** Medical claims · Diagnosing sleep apnea · Promising results

**Best product fits:** Sealy Luxury Hybrid, Midnight Bliss

---

## Back Pain

**Core problem:** Guests wake up stiff, sore, or uncomfortable.

**Messaging pillars:** Spinal support · Pressure relief · Better positioning · Lumbar support · Better mornings

**Strong angles:**
- The angle matters more than the mattress.
- Better support. Better mornings.
- Your back needs the right angle.

**Avoid:** Medical claims · Guaranteed pain relief

**Best product fits:** Midnight Bliss, Stearns Winslet

---

## Couples

**Core problem:** Two people sleep differently.

**Messaging pillars:** Independent adjustability · Personalized comfort · Better sleep for both partners · Less compromise

**Strong angles:**
- You don't have to sleep the same way.
- One bed. Two different sleepers.
- Better sleep together.

**Best product fits:** Stearns Winslet, Midnight Bliss

---

## Family

**Core problem:** Buying for loved ones with different comfort and mobility needs.

**Messaging pillars:** Comfort · Care · Recovery · Quality of life

**Strong angles:**
- Different needs. One solution.
- Comfort for every stage of life.
- Some gifts get used every day.

**Best product fits:** Stearns Winslet, Sealy Luxury Hybrid
