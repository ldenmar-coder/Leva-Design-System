## Tagging vocabulary

When adding assets, use lowercase kebab-case tags, semicolon-separated. Controlled values:

- **People:** people-none, people-solo, people-couple, people-family, people-hands
- **Mood:** mood-calm, mood-cozy, mood-intimate, mood-restful, mood-energized, mood-neutral
- **Light:** light-warm, light-bright, light-moody, light-natural
- **Time:** time-morning, time-day, time-evening, time-night
- **Setting:** set-bedroom, set-showroom, set-living, set-outdoor
- **Color:** color-purple, color-gold, color-cream, color-neutral
- **Campaign:** camp-demo-days, camp-founder, camp-72hr, camp-snoring, camp-back-pain, camp-couples, camp-brand
- **Season:** season-evergreen, season-holiday, season-winter, season-summer
